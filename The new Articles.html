ARTICLES

WHY?

Articles are a way of propogating news, results of a research, debate or academic analysis.

Some are angry, and use the word as a weapon against things that make them mad. Some are informed about something that has a message, and use words for empowerment of self or others. Some use it as a form of therapy and write journal-type articles which lay bare their symptoms. Some write them because it's a way to occasionally make some money with your skill or knowledge, or because it's a way to pay for your travel habit. Some people simply write them to express their love, appreciation, or criticism of the things they care about, like food, movements, literature, or whatever.

People write to express their thoughts and so, we have curated a collection of select articles from our friends.
